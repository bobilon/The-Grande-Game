# Sources and Methods

*What the work rests on, and where verification still belongs to the reader.*


This is a working cultural-studies document, not a footnoted scholarly apparatus. The structural claims in the seven strands rest on the kinds of sources listed below. I have not pinned every page number for every reference, and where a citation deserves verification before being relied on in a stronger publication, I note the kind of verification owed rather than fabricate a page reference. Anyone who wants to follow up has every name and date they need to do it.

### WHAT IS NOT IN THIS APPARATUS


There is a confidential channel — a private compass — that holds the identifications, the social-network claims, and the operational-architecture readings the public text does not name. That channel can be subpoenaed if a legal proceeding requires it. It is held by a third party in preserved form. It is not in this document and it is not on GitHub. The wall between the confidential channel and the public text is held throughout. The reader who wants to verify a structural claim has the public record; the reader who wants the identifications can ask the question through the channel a court can compel an answer in.
